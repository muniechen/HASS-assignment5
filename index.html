HASS-Assigment 5: Final Project Proposal

02.526 Interactive Data Visualization (2024)
Yuching, Cheng

Project Contents (what)

The recent earthquake has had a significant impact on Taiwan, causing various inconveniences in people's lives. Many people couldn't sleep well and keep worrying about the earthquake. The aim of this project is to showcase the magnitude of the earthquake and inform people to be careful when the upcoming earthquake occurs.

The data source used is a CENTRAL WEATHER ADMINISTRATION, which has been made public. It is a CSV document that records the time series seismological data.

Project Significance (why)

When the earthquake occurs, it's crucial to identify the epicenter. Following a significant earthquake, aftershocks typically occur around the epicenter. Therefore, knowing the epicenter is an important information that can alert residents in surrounding areas to the earthquake and provide them with more time to prepare for potential future earthquakes.

Through this visualization project, I aim to gain a more comprehensive understanding of earthquakes and extract valuable information to aid in future natural disaster prevention and control efforts.

Visualization methods (how)

1. Introduce the concept of earthquakes and the potential outcomes from earthquakes.

2. Create an interactive platform showing the plate junction actual positions in Taiwan, to demonstrate which areas have a higher possibility of experiencing earthquakes.

3. Prevention and preparedness.

4. Quick quiz: to test users if they have gained a complete understanding from the instructions.

5. Show the countdown earthquake system for upcoming earthquakes.

6. Show resources: Emergency contacts, additional readings, educational materials.

7. Footer: Email address or contact form for users to reach out with questions or feedback.
